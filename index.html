A bit about me

In this website you will find information about my professional career as well as my interests and skills developed along the way. In addition, you will be able to discover the projects I have been working on and I am proud to show. I know it is not easy to explain who I am in so little space. So take it as a little sip and if you have any questions or want to know more. Don't hesitate to contact me.

A person who loves to learn, to challenge myself and to improve every day. I love to come up with different scenarios and discover which ones are the most accurate. I translate my passions into my way of living and I like to fight for my dreams trying to make them come true.

I consider that I am a person with a fairly transversal career, I started studying business management with the desire to learn about the business world, along the way I realised that I fell in love with the technical side and the backend of business. When I discovered the world of software solutions, insights and data I couldn't stop growing and getting to know this sector.
